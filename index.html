Posematic â€” Motion Capture Data Loader

Posematic is an AI model that converts 2D sketches into 3D models. I developed a PyTorch data loader to convert motion capture datasets (e.g., CMU Mocap, Age Kinematics) into structured tensors for Posematic's training pipeline. The loader parsed skeletal hierarchies, extracted and normalized frame data, and efficiently batched motion sequences for model input to enable realistic motion learning from raw 3D data.
